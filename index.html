    • Puya Ghazizadeh is Assistant Professor of computer science at Division of Computer Science Mathematics and Science, St. John's University. Ghazizadeh completed his Ph.D. at the Old Dominion University in 2014.

    Presently Ghazizadeh serves as Co-Chair for the Fourth International Workshop on Mobile and Pervasive Internet of Things (PerIoT 2020) (co-located with IEEE PerCom 2020). He is acting as Co-Chair for the IEEE ITS Society Technical Committee: Intelligence and Security Information for Transportation Systems. Ghazizadeh served as Associate Editor for the The 22nd IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems 2019 (ITSC 2019).

    He is being a regular reviewer for several international journals and international conferences in the field of Mobile Computing, Intelligent Transportation Systems and Internet of Things (IoT).

    Ghazizadeh conducts research on Mobile Computing and Intelligent Transportation Systems focusing on Vehicular Clouds. Applications of vehicular cloud include content delivery network (CDN), traffic control and natural disaster management. His research interest also includes Database Security and Algorithm Optimization.

    He has widely published his research with more than 20 international conference articles and international journal articles.
