    • Dr. Puya Ghazizadeh is Assistant Professor at Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, Seton Hall University. Dr. Ghazizadeh conducts research on Intelligent Transportation Systems, focusing on Vehicular Clouds. Applications of vehicular cloud include traffic control and natural disaster management. His research interest also includes Database Security and Algorithm Optimization.

    He has widely published his research with more than 20 international conference articles and international journal articles.
