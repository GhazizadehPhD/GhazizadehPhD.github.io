    • Puya Ghazizadeh is Assistant Professor of computer science at Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, Seton Hall University.

    Presently Ghazizadeh serves as Co-Chair for the Third International Workshop on Mobile and Pervasive Internet of Things (PerIoT 2019) (co-located with IEEE PerCom 2019). He is acting as Co-Chair for the IEEE ITS Society Technical Committee: Intelligence and Security Information for Transportation Systems. Ghazizadeh served as Associate Editor for the The 21st IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems 2018 (ITSC 2018).

    He is being a regular reviewer for several international journals and international conferences in the field of Intelligent Transportation Systems and Internet of Things (IoT).

    Ghazizadeh conducts research on Mobile Computing and Intelligent Transportation Systems focusing on Vehicular Clouds. Applications of vehicular cloud include content delivery network (CDN), traffic control and natural disaster management. His research interest also includes Database Security and Algorithm Optimization.

    He has widely published his research with more than 20 international conference articles and international journal articles.
